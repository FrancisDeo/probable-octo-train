Nagtabon

If you’re looking for a beautiful beach near Puerto Princesa City, Nagtabon is the place to go to. It is less than an hour away from the city center, making it the ideal destination for those who don’t want to travel far to soak up some sun, sand, and beach time. It has white sand, an idyllic atmosphere, swaying coconut trees, and cool waters for swimming and skimboarding among other activities.

Taytay

The municipality has a land area of 1,257.68 square kilometers or 485.59 square miles which constitutes 8.59% of Palawan's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 83,357. This represented 8.87% of the total population of Palawan province, or 2.58% of the overall population of the MIMAROPA Region. Based on these figures, the population density is computed at 66 inhabitants per square kilometer or 172 inhabitants per square mile.

Banaue Rice Terraces

Ang Hagdan-hagdang Palayan ng Banawe (Ingles: Banaue Rice Terraces) ay mga 2000-taong gulang na mga hagdanang-taniman na nililok sa mga bulubundukin ng Ifugao sa Pilipinas ng mga ninuno ng mga katutubong mamamayanang Batad. Karaniwang tinatawag ito ng mga Pilipino bilang "Ikawalong Kahangahangang Pook sa Mundo". Tinatawag itong payew sa katutubong pananalita sa Ifugao.
